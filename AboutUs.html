— Our Story —

Located in the heart of the Mediterranean Sea, The Italian Job restaurant combines comfort, coziness and high quality cuisine.

Traditional Italian recipes intertwined with non-standard combinations of tastes and techniques, prepared with care, creativity and love.



— Authentic Italian Cuisine —

Inspired by the dynamics of the city and the diversity of people, in the Italian Job Restaurant we strive to be different.

With care for you we select the best products coming to us directly from Italy.
